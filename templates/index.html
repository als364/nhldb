Penalties vs Wins

Calculates how likely an NHL team is to win a game in which they have a fight vs a game in which they do not have a fight. 'Fight' is defined as any fighting or roughing penalty on either team during the game.
The NHL didn't keep track of penalty data before the 1979-1980 season, so data only extends that far. Sorry, Detroit Falcons and Toronto Arenas fans.
